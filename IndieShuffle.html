How it works

IndieShuffle is built to be simple. No complex controls, no learning curve – just a quick way to inject fresh indie tracks into your day.

1

Add IndieShuffle to Chrome

Install the extension from the Chrome Web Store in a few seconds. A small icon will appear next to your address bar.

2

Open the popup

Click the IndieShuffle icon whenever you want new music. You’ll see today’s 10 tracks waiting for you.

3

Press play, discover, repeat

Listen through, save what you like, skip what you don’t. Tomorrow you’ll get a brand-new batch.

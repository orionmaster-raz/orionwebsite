Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

IndieShuffle is a Google Chrome extension designed to help users discover newly released music from publicly available online sources.

Information We Collect

IndieShuffle does not collect, store, or sell personally identifiable information.

The extension may process the following data locally on the user’s device:

  • Anonymous usage counters (e.g., free vs. premium feature limits)
  • Extension preferences and configuration settings

This data remains on the user’s device unless required to enable paid features.

Payments

Payments for IndieShuffle premium features are processed securely by Stripe. IndieShuffle does not store or process payment card details.

Third-Party Services

IndieShuffle may use third-party services strictly to enable core functionality (such as payment processing). No personal data is shared for advertising purposes.

Data Sharing

IndieShuffle does not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties.

Data Security

Reasonable technical measures are used to protect the extension and its functionality. However, no software system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Children’s Privacy

IndieShuffle is not intended for use by children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
